# Document Templates

## Quick 1-Pager Template (Decision-Focused)

Use this for fast evaluation and decision-making. Keep to exactly 1 page.

---

**[Product/Feature Name]**

**Customer & Problem**
[2-3 sentences: Who is the specific customer? What problem do they have? Why does it matter?]

**Proposed Solution**
[2-3 sentences: What's the end-state customer experience? What changes for them?]

**Key Benefit**
[1 sentence: The single most important reason a customer would choose this]

**Critical Assumptions** (Mark each: High/Medium/Low Risk)
1. [Assumption about customer need/behavior] - Risk: [H/M/L] - Validate by: [method]
2. [Assumption about solution/feasibility] - Risk: [H/M/L] - Validate by: [method]  
3. [Assumption about business viability] - Risk: [H/M/L] - Validate by: [method]

**What We Know vs Don't Know**
- Evidence we have: [List what's validated]
- Evidence we need: [List top 3 gaps]

**Competitive Context**
[2-3 sentences: How do customers solve this today? Why is this 10x better, not 10%?]

**Critical Risks**
- [What could kill this idea?]
- [What's the biggest risk to customer adoption?]
- [What's the biggest risk to business viability?]

**Recommendation: [BUILD / INVESTIGATE / PIVOT / KILL]**
[2-3 sentences: Clear recommendation with reasoning. What should happen next?]

**Next Steps** (if recommending BUILD or INVESTIGATE)
1. [Immediate action]
2. [Second priority]
3. [Third priority]
